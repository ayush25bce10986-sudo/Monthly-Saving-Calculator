# Statement Document

## Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle to track their monthly income, expenses, and
savings effectively. Without a clear understanding of where their money
goes, planning for financial goals becomes difficult. This program
provides a simple way to calculate monthly savings and estimate how long
it will take to reach a savings goal.

## Scope of the Project

-   The project covers calculations related to income, expenses, and
    savings.
-   It computes total expenses, monthly savings, and savings rate.
-   It includes a savings goal calculator to estimate the time required
    to achieve a financial target.
-   It is limited to command-line interaction and basic arithmetic
    calculations.

## Target Users

-   Students who want to manage their pocket money.
-   Working professionals who wish to track monthly budgets.
-   Anyone looking for a basic savings and budgeting tool.
-   Beginners learning Python and exploring simple financial
    applications.

## High-Level Features

-   Input-based monthly income and expense collection.
-   Automated calculation of total expenses and savings.
-   Savings rate percentage calculation.
-   Savings goal estimator with month and year breakdown.
-   Clear text-based interface suitable for beginners.
